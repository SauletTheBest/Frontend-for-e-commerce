Car Brands from Cyberpunk 2077

  • Archer – One of the most popular brands for ordinary city dwellers. Archer specialises in producing affordable and accessible cars for the middle class. Their models, such as the Hella EC-D, are known for their simplicity and reliability, ideal for urban travelling.
  • Makigai – Known for its compact and fuel-efficient cars. Makigai produces low-cost vehicles for ordinary workers and students, as well as electric motorbikes. The MaiMai P126 has become synonymous with urban minimalism and affordability.
  • Rayfield – Rayfield is the brand for those who want to show their status. Their cars are equipped with the most advanced technology and offer unrivalled comfort. The Caliburn is the pinnacle of luxury and speed, designed for those who can afford the best.
  • Quadra – Quadra is an iconic American manufacturer of musclecars and sports cars. Their cars, such as the Quadra Turbo-R V-Tech, are associated with speed, power and aggressive styling. It is the choice for racers and those who appreciate power and style in one package.
  • Thorton – A brand specialising in off-road vehicles and trucks. Thorton is known for its reliable and practical vehicles for work purposes and cross-country travelling. It is the choice for those who value functionality and reliability in the hustle and bustle of urban or wasteland environments.
  • Mizutani – Japanese brand specialising in sports and racing cars. Mizutani produces high-performance cars that combine technological innovation and speed. The Shion ‘Coyote’ model is designed for fast driving on open roads, offering drivers a thrilling experience.
  • Villefort – Villefort specialises in large and powerful vehicles including SUVs, sedans and trucks. Their vehicles are used for both business and personal use. For example, the Alvarado model is often used as vehicles for important business meetings and cargo transport.
